Project Details

When I transferred into the Game Design degree, one of this first classes I took was the study of U.I in video games. We spent 10 weeks studying how different games use U.I to convey information and how one could use their target audience in order to specifically design U.I to appeal to them through subtle details. These details being font, color, aesthetic, and overall organization of information and buttons.

Our final project for this class was to take 2 screenshots of UI from video games and redesign it to improve it on all levels (appeal to target audience, make user experience smoother, etc.). So for my project, I chose to take 2 screens from No Man's Sky and redesign it to have a more sci-fi aesthetic in addition to adding many more U.I mechanics that I think the game should've had.
