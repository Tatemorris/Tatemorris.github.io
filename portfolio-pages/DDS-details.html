Project Details

For the final project of my video game scripting course, we had to use what we learned throughout the 10 weeks of learning to create a video game utilizing a wide variety of mechanics.

So for my game, Duck, Duck, Shoot, I created a first-person shooter that had elements of raycasting, a static game manager, enemy NAV AI, enemy patrol and attack states, collectibles that restored health, phsyics, sound, and UI that included a pause menu, game over menu, and game end screen.

The story was that Geese ran a corporation that profitted off the demise of Ducks, so you play as a mallard who busts the door of the building, clearing every floor of geese to take revenge for what they did to their people. This was the first full 3D game I've made that had this many mechanics, scripts, and proper beginning and end all in Unity.
