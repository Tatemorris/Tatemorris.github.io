Hello! My name is Tate Morris and I am currently a Game Design & Production student at Drexel University! I am looking for a position in the field of video game programming & with a special interest in gameplay mechanics.

I have been wanting to enter the field of game design since 7th grade. It all started when I played Skyrim for the first time. At this point in my life, I wanted to figure out what career I might want so that when I start high school, I could set myself up to get into the right college with the right program. I had gone through several ideas, like astronomy, medical research, etc. But then it hit me when I encountered a game breaking glitch. I realized video games, just like any other application, were written with code. I’m good with technology; I love computers, I love video games, and most importantly, I love making people laugh and smile. Mix all these interests together, and you get the world of video game development.

Since then I have been doing everything I could to pave the way to successful game development career. Taking as many game coding classes as I could, going to a 5 day long summer camp using UE4 blueprints to make a game, and now that I am at Drexel Univserity, I am dedicated to working for a gaming company every co-op so that I would gain 18 months of incredible experiences while getting a degree in Game Design.
