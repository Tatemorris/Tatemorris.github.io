Hello! My name is Tate Morris and I am currently a Game Design student at Champlain College! I am a passionate game designer wanting to specialize in gameplay mechanics for either video or tabletop board games!

My interest in game development started in middle school when I first played The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim and ran into a game-breaking glitch. It clicked that games, like any software, are built through systems and code. Since then, I’ve been drawn to the intersection of technology, design, and player experience—and I enjoy creating work that helps people laugh, engage, and feel something.

Since then, I’ve pursued every opportunity I can to prepare for a career in game development: taking as many design and programming courses as possible, attending an intensive five-day Unreal Engine 4 Blueprint summer camp, and building projects that strengthen my fundamentals. With each project, my passion for both tabletop and digital games continues to grow—and I’m excited to bring that energy into the game industry.
